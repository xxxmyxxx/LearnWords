Learn Words App Account and Data Deletion Process

Data Deletion Process

To permanently delete your account and data, you can follow these steps:

  1. Go to "Settings" in the app
  2. Click on "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm the deletion

Alternatively, you can send your account deletion request to the following email address:

support@learnwordsapp.com

Data to be Deleted

After the account deletion process, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile and identification information
  • Learning history and statistics
  • In-app settings and preferences
  • Facebook connection and associated data

Important Notes

  • The deletion process cannot be undone
  • All your data will be completely deleted within 30 days
  • Data required to be kept due to legal obligations is not affected by this process
