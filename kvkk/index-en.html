GDPR Information

Data Controller

At Learn Words App, we take the utmost care in protecting your personal data. In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), as the data controller, your personal data may be processed within the scope of the purposes explained below.

Purpose of Personal Data Processing

  • Creation and management of user accounts
  • Personalization of in-app learning experience
  • Statistical analysis
  • Fulfillment of legal obligations

Processed Personal Data

  • Name, surname
  • Email address
  • User learning statistics
  • App usage data

Transfer of Personal Data

Your personal data may be shared with third parties only to the extent necessary, in line with our legal obligations and service requirements.

Rights of the Data Subject

Under Article 15 of the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to know if your personal data is being processed
  • Right to request information about the processing of your personal data
  • Right to know the purpose of data processing and whether it is used in accordance with this purpose
  • Right to request correction or deletion of your personal data

Contact

To exercise your rights under GDPR, you can send an email to support@learnwords.store.
